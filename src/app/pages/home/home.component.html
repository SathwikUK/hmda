Welcome to Kothwalguda Eco Park — A Green Escape

Kothwalguda Eco Park is a beautiful green oasis, offering boating, cycling, gardens, and more. Escape the hustle of the city and enjoy fresh air, serene lakes, and lush gardens.

About Kothwalguda Eco Park

Spanning 125 acres on the scenic Himayat Sagar lakefront, Kothwalguda Eco Park is Hyderabad's premier eco-tourism destination, designed to offer visitors an unforgettable experience with nature.

India's Largest Aquarium

Experience marine life like never before at India's largest aquarium featuring diverse aquatic species and immersive displays.

Largest Aviary

Marvel at exotic birds from around the world in our expansive aviary, offering an up-close experience with nature's finest.

Butterfly Garden

Stroll through our enchanting butterfly garden and sensory park, designed to delight visitors of all ages.

Adventure Zone

Thrill-seekers can enjoy our adventure zone with exciting activities, including a suspension bridge and boardwalks.

Luxury Accommodations

Stay overnight in our luxury wooden cottages and resorts with infinity pools and modern amenities.

Family Activities

Enjoy boating, cycling, camping, and various recreational activities perfect for families and friends.

Perfect for Every Occasion

Whether you're planning a family outing, romantic getaway, corporate event, or adventure trip, Kothwalguda Eco Park offers something special for everyone. Come experience the harmony of nature and modern amenities.
