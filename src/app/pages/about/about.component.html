About Kothwalguda Eco Park

Where Nature Meets Conservation

Established 2020

India's Premier Eco-Tourism Destination

Spread across 125 acres of lush greenery, Kothwalguda Eco Park stands as a testament to sustainable tourism and environmental conservation in the heart of Hyderabad.

Our Vision

To create a harmonious space where visitors can connect with nature while contributing to environmental preservation. We believe in educating our guests about biodiversity, conservation, and the importance of ecological balance.

What Makes Us Special

  • India's Largest Aquarium: Home to over 200 species of marine life
  • Butterfly Conservatory: A tropical paradise with 50+ butterfly species
  • Exotic Aviary: Housing rare and exotic birds from around the world
  • Adventure Activities: Zipline, rock climbing, and rope courses
  • Nature Trails: 5km of walking paths through diverse ecosystems
  • Sustainable Practices: Solar powered, rainwater harvesting, and waste recycling

Conservation Efforts

At Kothwalguda Eco Park, we're committed to protecting and preserving our natural heritage. Our conservation programs focus on habitat restoration, wildlife protection, and environmental education. We work closely with local communities and environmental organizations to ensure the long-term sustainability of our ecosystem.
